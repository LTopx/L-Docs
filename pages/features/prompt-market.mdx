# Prompt Market

Plan to develop a Prompt marketplace. A good prompt can unleash greater potential in AI. When you have an excellent prompt, it can help you get the desired answers faster and further improve productivity.

### Sync excellent prompt library

Sync excellent prompts from [awesome-chatgpt-prompts](https://github.com/f/awesome-chatgpt-prompts)

### User-created

Allow users to create their own prompts. You can also share prompts that you think are excellent, upload them to the dedicated Prompt Market for L-GPT, and provide them for more people to use.
