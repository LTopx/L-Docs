# Text To Speech

### Introduction

Through the Azure TTS service, L-GPT can convert the text content returned by AI into speech. This feature is only available to premium users.
![new-step-1.png](/features/tts/preview-en.png)

### More settings

- You can also adjust the voice tone of the speech, currently supporting dozens of different tones.
  ![new-step-1.png](/features/tts/voice-en.png)
- You can also adjust the speech speed and enable automatic playback.
  ![new-step-1.png](/features/tts/others-en.png)
