# Login

Currently, the following login methods are supported.

![login](/change-log/v0.9.1-1-en.png)

### Github / Google

Log in with your own Github or Google account.

### Email

Log in with your email account. When logging in, a login email will be sent to the email address you provided. Click on the link in the email to complete the login.

![1](/basic/login/1-en.png)
![2](/basic/login/2.png)

### Account (Email) / Password

- All passwords are stored encrypted using MD5.
- First, you need to configure an initial password by going to "Account Center - Change Password" after logging in.
- Then enter your email and password on the login page.
