## What is Le-AI

Le-AI is an open-source third-party client based on the **OpenAI API**. It is developed using **Next.js** and packaged as a client with the help of **Tauri**.

Link: <Link href={env.NEXT_PUBLIC_WEB_LINK} className="text-[#006be6] dark:text-[#008ae6] underline" target="_blank">{env.NEXT_PUBLIC_WEB_LINK}</Link>
